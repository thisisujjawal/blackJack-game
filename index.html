BlackJack Rules:

This is a popular BlackJack Game also known as 21 Game.

AIM:

Basically the game is all about who's score will get closer to 21.

Cards Values:

All the number cards are have the same value as number on the card. On getting ACE card.. you will be given a choice of getting either 1 or 11 score. Remaining face cards have value of 10
